AI Privacy Report covers the intersection of AI systems and privacy law — GDPR enforcement against AI products, training-data provenance, data subject access requests for model outputs, and emerging regulatory frameworks that affect AI deployment.

## What we cover

- **GDPR and CCPA enforcement** — regulatory actions involving AI products, with case analysis
- **Training data provenance** — data subject rights, consent requirements, and sourcing compliance
- **EU AI Act** — compliance requirements, prohibited uses, risk classification, and implementation timelines
- **Privacy engineering** — technical patterns for GDPR-compliant ML pipelines and data minimization
- **Regulatory tracker** — enforcement actions, guidance documents, and proposed legislation

## Who reads this

Privacy professionals, legal engineers, compliance teams, and ML practitioners who need to understand what regulations actually require of AI systems — translated from legal language into actionable technical guidance.

## Stay current

Subscribe to the RSS feed for regulatory updates and enforcement coverage. This publication provides analysis, not legal advice — consult qualified legal counsel for specific compliance decisions.
